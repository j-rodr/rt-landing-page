How our scores work

Every film or television show receives two scores, one from critics and the other one from the audience (registered users). Both scores can be either Fresh or Rotten depending on the percentage of positive reviews.

Rotten Tomatoes has assembled a team of curators whose job it is to read thousands of movie and TV reviews weekly. The team collects eviews from Tomatometer-approved critics and publications every day, generating Tomatometer scores.
